Frequently Asked

Questions

What different types of categories does Balm offer me?

Balm offers you some of the following categories: Sleep, Meditation and Music.

Each section contains about 11 subcategories for sounds like Nature and ASMR (among many others).

What is the difference between the free trial and the premium version?

The main difference is that the free trial version will allow you to use the app for 7 days and play/download a limited amount of sounds. On the other hand, the premium version offers you a monthly or yearly fee, giving you the option to select how much time you will want to use the app. On top of that, it also gives you unlimited access to all the exclusive content on the app.

Will the app offer more sections in the future?

Yes, we are always updating Balm with new sections and sounds to give our users the best experience possible.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, Balm works with or without an internet connection. To hear sounds without an internet connection remember to download them on the app.
